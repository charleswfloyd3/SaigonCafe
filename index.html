About Us

Saigon Cafe is a family run vietnamese and chinese cuisine restuarant located in the Provo Downtown Area serving Utah Valley Area since 2001. With over 1,000 reviews and 4.5 stars, we're proud to say that we make the best asian cuisine in Provo. Saigon is a great place to bring your friends and family. We are currently only open for delivery and takeout, but will reopem for indoor dining starting June 2nd. Give us a shot and you'll be hooked!
